FAQ
Send Raven
To send Ravencoin, enter the address (account) of the recipient. Enter the amount of Ravencoin you'd like to send. Enter some text in the optional memo field. The info in the memo field does not get sent, and is for your reference only.
Receive Raven
To receive Ravencoin, the other party needs to know your Ravencoin public address (account). They can scan your barcode, or you can copy/paste your address into an e-mail, or have your phone send the address via e-mail, or text.
Set PIN
Set a PIN number that you'll remember. This will be required to send Raven, and to view your recovery phrase.
Touch ID
Turn this on to require biometric authentication to spend. You can enable a spending limit in the Touch ID Spending Limit screen.
Paper Key
This will help you write down 12 words. These 12 words are enough to recover your funds. The 12 words create a seed from which lots of Raven addresses can be created. Store your 12 words very safely, and do not allow others to know the words.
Import Wallet
Import wallet will transfer the funds from a paper wallet, or a private key. The funds will be transferred from the other address to your Raven wallet address.
Start (wipe) Wallet
Starting a new wallet will wipe out the old wallet. After you start a new wallet, you can enter your 12 word recovery phrase for a new wallet. Provided you always have your 12 word recovery phrase for all of your wallets, you can use RVN wallet for more than one wallet.
