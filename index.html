FAQ
Send Raven
To send Ravencoin, enter the address (account) of the recipient. Enter the amount of Ravencoin you'd like to send. Enter some text in the optional memo field. The info in the memo field does not get sent, and is for your reference only.
Receive Raven
To receive Ravencoin, the other party needs to know your Ravencoin public address (account). They can scan your barcode, or you can copy/paste your address into an e-mail, or have your phone send the address via e-mail, or text.
Set PIN
Set a PIN number that you'll remember. This will be required to send Raven, and to view your recovery phrase.
Touch ID
Touch ID is currently inactive.
Paper Key
This will help you write down 12 words. These 12 words are enough to recover your funds. The 12 words create a seed from which lots of Raven addresses can be created. Store your 12 words very safely, and do not allow others to know the words.
Import Wallet
Import wallet will transfer the funds from a paper wallet, or a private key. The funds will be transferred from the other address to your Raven wallet address.
Start / Recover Wallet
Starting or recovering another wallet allows you to access and manage a different Ravencoin Wallet on this device.

Your current wallet will be removed from this device. If you wish to restore it in the future you will need to enter your Paper Key.
Display Currency
Displays the currency in full Ravens, or micro Ravens. Micro Ravens are one millionth of a Raven.

You can also display the fiat currency value of the Ravens in your wallet.
Sync Blockchain
Syncs the blockchain. Use this if the transaction shows completed on the Raven blockchain, but not in your wallet.
Buy Raven
If you can't mine Raven, you can buy it from others that have mined it.

Raven trades on decentralized and centralized exchanges.

Crypto-Bridge is a decentralized exchange which uses a progam (and a blockchain) to pair up matching orders and execute trades.

Nanex is a centralized exchange.

Other exchanges will come online later. Visit the Raven Wiki for more info.
Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How do I buy Raven?
A: Visit CryptoBridge or Nanex.

Q: What is Ravencoin?
A: Visit Ravencoin.org to learn more about Ravencoin.
Privacy Policy
We, the developers of RVN wallet, do not have access to your private information or actively track you in any way.

Your private keys are only ever accessible to your own device. If you keep your 12 word phrase secret, then only your device has access to your private keys.

Users may install and use the RVN wallet without entering any private information.

Any emails we receive to customer support, or feedback will be visible to our employees.
